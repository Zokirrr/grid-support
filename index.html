FAQ

  • How do I change my life expectancy?
    Open Settings → Life Expectancy and choose 50/60/70/80/90/100.
  • Why can’t I proceed after opening the app?
    Tap “hello”, select your date of birth, then tap Continue.
  • How do reminders work?
    Enable reminders in Settings. You’ll receive a weekend notification showing weeks left.

Privacy

Grid does not sell or share your data. Notes are stored on your device. If you sign in with Apple, data is associated with your Apple ID.
